# When you get home

The first thing to do is to reset the WiFi settings to reconfigure it for your own WiFi.

With the star powered on, hold down the right hand button labelled *SW1* then press and release the *RESET* button. Keep pressing *SW1* for about 10 seconds.

![reset](../img/athome.jpg)

Proceed to the previous [setup instructions](configurewled.md)
